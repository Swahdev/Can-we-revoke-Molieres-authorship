- what is stylometry? 
Stylometry is the study of authorship attribution, which is the process of identifying the author of a text.
It is a subfield of literary forensics, and is also known as stylometrics, authorship identification,
authorship attribution, author identification, authorship verification, authorship analysis, 
author profiling, authorship forensics, or authorship fingerprinting.

it uses NLTK (natural langage Toolkit) , a library for natural language processing, and scikit-learn, a machine learning library for python.
